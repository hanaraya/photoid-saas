# U.S. Passport Photo Compliance Rules

> Reference for validation logic. Source: Official DOS requirements.

## Photo Format
- Size: exactly 2 x 2 inches (51 x 51 mm)
- Color photo only
- Square (1:1 aspect ratio)
- High resolution, no blur or pixelation

## Head & Framing ⚠️ CRITICAL
- **Entire head must be visible** from top of head (hair included) to bottom of chin
- **Top of head MUST NOT be cropped**
- Head height (chin to crown): 1 to 1⅜ inches (25–35 mm)
- Head must occupy 50–69% of photo height
- Head must be centered
- Head must be straight (no tilt)

## Face & Expression
- Neutral expression only
- Mouth closed
- Eyes open and fully visible
- Subject must look directly at the camera

## Eyes & Accessories
- Eyeglasses NOT allowed
- No sunglasses or tinted lenses
- No headphones, earbuds, or devices
- Jewelry allowed only if it does not obscure face

## Head Coverings
- Not allowed unless religious or medical
- If allowed, face must be fully visible
- No shadows from coverings

## Background & Lighting
- Plain white or off-white background
- No shadows on face or background
- Even lighting
- Natural skin tone

## Photo Editing
- No filters
- No retouching
- No AI enhancement
- No background replacement

## Clothing
- Normal daily attire
- No uniforms
- No camouflage patterns

## Child Exceptions
- Infant eyes may be closed
- No hands, toys, or supports visible
