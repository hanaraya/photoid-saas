### Section 2: Official Requirements (400 words)
**H2: US Passport Photo Requirements 2026**

- Size: 2x2 inches (51x51mm)
- Head size: 1-1 3/8 inches from chin to top
- Background: White or off-white
- Recent: Within last 6 months
- Expression: Neutral, eyes open
- Glasses: NOT allowed (as of 2016)
- Head coverings: Only for religious reasons

### Section 3: Step-by-Step Guide (600 words)
**H2: How to Take Your Passport Photo: Step-by-Step**

#### Step 1: Set Up Your Background
- Find plain white wall
- Remove pictures/decorations from frame
- Alternative: Hang white sheet or poster board
- Check for shadows

#### Step 2: Lighting Setup
- Best: Face a window (natural daylight)
- Time: Midday for even lighting
- Avoid: Direct sunlight (harsh shadows)
- Alternative: Two lamps on either side

#### Step 3: Camera Position
- Distance: 4-6 feet away
- Height: Eye level
- Use timer or ask someone to help
- Front camera works but rear is better quality

#### Step 4: Your Position
- Face camera directly
- Shoulders square
- Chin level (not tilted)
- Neutral expression (slight smile okay)
- Eyes open, looking at camera

#### Step 5: Take Multiple Shots
- Take 10-15 photos
- Check each for:
  - Eyes open?
  - Face centered?
  - No shadows?
  - Background clean?

**H2: 7 Passport Photo Mistakes That Get Applications Rejected**

1. **Shadows on face** - Use even lighting
2. **Wrong background** - Must be white/off-white

4. **Wrong size/cropping** - Use our tool to fix
5. **Old photo** - Must be within 6 months
6. **Smile too big** - Keep neutral
7. **Looking away** - Eyes must face camera
